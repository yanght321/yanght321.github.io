I'm currently a research engineer at Kuaishou Technology, working on exciting projects related to visual content creation. Before joining Kuaishou, I received the M.S. degree (under the supervision of Xun Cao and Hao Zhu) and B.S. degree from Nanjing University, in 2021 and 2018 respectively.

My research interests lie in computer vision and computer graphics, including deep generative models, video generation and editing, human digitization, and 3D vision.

We are looking for self-motivated interns specializing in video synthesis and editing. Please feel free to contact me via email if you are interested.
